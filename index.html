System Optimization

The 95% Latency Kill (The "5-Minute Problem")

The Problem

A synchronous route was processing 5MB+ payloads and 10k+ records, blocking the Node.js event loop for 5 minutes (300 seconds).

The Solution

Refactored to an asynchronous chunked processing pipeline with parallelized batches and integrated field-level encryption.

95% REDUCTION

Latency dropped from 300 seconds to 15 seconds.
